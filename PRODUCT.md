# Product Design Context
<!-- generated-by: /upgrade-project -->

## Who we're designing for

**Job seekers (user role)** — people actively building and refining their CV and cover letter to apply for jobs. They want a polished, professional document fast. They are comfortable with digital tools but not necessarily designers.

**HR / Recruiters (admin role, invited only)** — administrators who manage CV/LM templates. They care about template quality, consistency, and ATS compliance.

> V2 persona envisaged: `recruiter` for B2B extension.

## Brand voice

Direction artistique : **"Papier & Crayon"** — the interface should feel like a high-quality blank sheet of paper. Precise, artisanal, calm.

- Tone: professional, reassuring, efficient — never loud or flashy
- Voice: direct and minimal — let the document speak, not the interface
- Key metaphors: pencil stroke, ivory paper, handcrafted care

## Anti-references (styles to avoid)

- No neomorphism
- No glassmorphism
- No bright colors or neon gradients
- No heavy drop shadows or dramatic 3D effects
- No gradient fills
- No solid-fill icons (line-art only, stroke 1.5px via Lucide Icons)
- No spectacular animations — transitions max 150–200ms, subtle only
